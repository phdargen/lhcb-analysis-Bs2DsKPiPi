% Abstract -----------------------------------------------
\begin{abstract}
  \noindent
We present the measurement of the branching fraction of decay $\Bs\to\Ds\kaon\pion\pion$ using the complete 3 $\invfb$ of data, collected during Run 1 of the LHC. 
The branching fraction is measured relative to the decay $\Bs\to\Ds\pion\pion\pion$, from which we obtain

\begin{equation*}
\frac{\mathcal{B}(\Bs\to\Ds\kaon\pion\pion)}{\mathcal{B}(\Bs\to\Ds\pion\pion\pion)}= 0.051 \pm 0.002 \pm 0.002
\end{equation*}

The $\Bs\to\Ds\kaon\pion\pion$ decay can be further used to measure the weak CKM phase $\gamma$ in a time-dependent analysis of the $\Bs$ and $\Bsb$ decay rates. 
This will be the final goal of the presented analysis.  

\end{abstract}
